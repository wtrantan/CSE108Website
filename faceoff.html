USA vs Canada Showdown - February 15, 2025

Today’s game between USA and Canada in the 4 Nations Faceoff was intense, with USA securing a 3-1 victory. The match was highly physical, featuring multiple fights in the first nine seconds.

Connor McDavid scored for Canada, while Dylan Larkin and Jake Guentzel scored for the USA. This victory marks the end of Canada’s 17-game winning streak in best-on-best competition.
